Our Story

Founded on the principle that textile care should nourish, not compromise

lïnn emerged from a fundamental observation: conventional fabric softeners prioritize immediate softness over long-term wellness. We sought to create an alternative—one that respects the integrity of natural fibers while supporting holistic health.

Every formulation begins with botanical ingredients, carefully selected for their affinity with skin and fabric alike. We draw inspiration from Mediterranean traditions of textile care, where sun-dried linens and aromatic herbs have defined quality for generations.

Our approach is deliberate, unhurried. We believe in transparency of ingredient, clarity of purpose, and the quiet confidence that comes from creating something genuinely considered.

"True softness emerges from care, not chemistry."

— Mathew Godinez, Founder
Our Philosophy

Six principles that guide our formulations

Plant-based composition

+

We source botanical ingredients exclusively—no synthetic fragrances, no petrochemical derivatives. Each component is selected for its proven efficacy and gentle interaction with skin. Our formulas respect the body's natural chemistry, working in harmony rather than opposition.

Concentrated formula

+

Minimal water, maximum efficacy. Our concentrated approach reduces packaging, lowers shipping impact, and ensures each bottle delivers sustained performance. A measured capful suffices for even the largest loads—economy through considered design.

Dermatologically tested

+

Hypoallergenic and pH-balanced for sensitive skin. Our formulations undergo rigorous dermatological testing to ensure compatibility across skin types, including those prone to eczema and dermatitis. Safe for infants, gentle for all.

Fiber preservation

+

Advanced botanical polymers coat fibers without compromising breathability or absorbency. Colors remain vibrant, fabrics retain their original hand-feel. We extend the lifespan of your textiles through chemistry that respects material integrity.

Lasting fragrance

+

Essential oil complexes provide subtle, enduring scent without overwhelming. Our fragrances develop gradually, revealing different notes throughout the day—an olfactory experience informed by perfumery tradition, never synthetic imitation.

Sustainable packaging

+

Post-consumer recycled materials form our bottles, themselves fully recyclable. We've eliminated unnecessary secondary packaging, reduced bottle weight, and partnered with ocean plastic recovery initiatives. Every purchase contributes to marine conservation efforts.

Scent Collection

Four distinct compositions, each a study in botanical harmony

01

Lavender Twilight

Calming · Herbaceous · Warm

French lavender absolute meets warm vanilla bean, grounded by earthy vetiver. Evokes the stillness of Provençal fields at dusk—purple blooms releasing their fragrance as heat gives way to evening coolness.

02

Coastal Morning

Fresh · Mineral · Crisp

Sea salt and sun-bleached linen, brightened by bergamot and coastal sage. The scent of Mediterranean mornings—washing hung to dry in salted air, stone walls warming under early light.

03

Garden at Dawn

Floral · Green · Delicate

Jasmine and wild chamomile bloom among dewy grasses, softened by white musk. A hidden garden in the Greek countryside, where morning reveals overnight flowering and the earth smells of renewal.

04

Citrus Grove

Bright · Aromatic · Verdant

Amalfi lemon and bitter orange, layered with fresh basil and mint. The vitality of Italian coastal groves—fruit-laden branches overhead, herb gardens below, sun filtering through leaves.
