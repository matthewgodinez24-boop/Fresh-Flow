Our Story

Why vinegar?

lïnn emerged from a simple question: why do we soften our clothes with chemicals when nature has already perfected the solution? For generations, people used white vinegar as a natural fabric softener—effective, gentle, and pure. We brought this wisdom back, refined it, and packaged it for modern life.

Vinegar's natural acetic acid works at a molecular level to relax fabric fibers, strip away detergent residue, and neutralize odors. No synthetic fragrances masking smells. No petroleum-based softening agents coating your clothes. Just clean, soft textiles that breathe the way they're meant to.

The seed oil problem

Most fabric softeners rely on seed oils and synthetic derivatives—cheap fillers that coat fabrics, trap odors, and irritate sensitive skin. These ingredients break down into microplastics, harm waterways, and create dependency on harsh chemicals to maintain softness.

We took a different path. Every ingredient in lïnn is plant-derived, biodegradable, and completely free from seed oils and synthetics. What you get is genuine softness—not a chemical coating masquerading as care.

Holistic health starts with what touches your skin

Your clothes are in constant contact with your body. What softens them matters. We formulated lïnn for people who understand that holistic health isn't just about what you eat—it's about every product that enters your home.

Hypoallergenic. Dermatologically tested. Safe for the most sensitive skin. Free from everything artificial. This is fabric care that aligns with how you want to live.
